Core Technologies

Explore the groundbreaking innovations that set ECO DRIVE apart.

EcoPower Drivetrain

Our revolutionary drivetrain system achieves unprecedented efficiency through advanced materials and innovative engineering. With dual-motor configuration and adaptive power distribution, EcoPower delivers exceptional performance while minimizing energy consumption.

The system's regenerative capabilities recover up to 70% of kinetic energy during braking, significantly extending range during city driving.

  • Up to 95% energy conversion efficiency
  • Intelligent torque vectoring for enhanced handling
  • Adaptive power distribution based on driving conditions
  • Thermal management system for optimal performance

NexGen Battery Technology

Our proprietary solid-state battery technology represents a quantum leap in energy storage. With higher energy density and faster charging capabilities, NexGen batteries deliver exceptional range without compromising safety or longevity.

The innovative cell architecture and thermal management system ensure optimal performance in all conditions, from arctic cold to desert heat.

  • 500+ mile range on a single charge
  • 80% charge in just 15 minutes
  • 10-year/500,000-mile warranty
  • Zero thermal runaway risk for unmatched safety

EcoSmart OS

Our intelligent operating system connects all vehicle systems into a seamless, intuitive experience. EcoSmart OS continuously learns from your driving habits to optimize performance, comfort, and efficiency.

With over-the-air updates, your ECO DRIVE vehicle improves over time, gaining new features and enhanced capabilities without requiring dealer visits.

  • AI-driven energy optimization for maximum efficiency
  • Predictive maintenance to prevent issues before they occur
  • Personalized driving experience that adapts to your preferences
  • Seamless integration with smart home and personal devices
